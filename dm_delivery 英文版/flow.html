Inquiry

Order Confirmation

Remittance
  After confirm payment, your order will be confirmed. Please notice, cancellation will not be acceptable.
Design Data Transfer
  After you transfer all data to us, we will mail you a notice mail and notice that your data is arrival.
  Please notice, we will not change any data. We will use original data for final printing.

  Please notice, any destroy of file will influence our work and delay delivery time.
  Please notice, we will not take responsibility because of data destroy.
Color Correction
  Color correction service is using professional printing machine for correction.
  You can use this service for final color correction, but it is extra payment service. (Recommended)
DM Printing / Member name label sticker printing
  After confirm correction of printing data and member name label data, we will use these data to start our printing, input printing to envelop, seal.
 
Delivery
  According to UPU regulations, mail sent abroad, the quantity of delivery to other country’s post office is limited.
  Japan is divided into 3 big areas, Tokyo, Osaka, Okinawa.
  For example:（〒*口口*口-口口口口口)
*口口* = necessary part. Tokyo: 01-51，Osaka: 52-93，Okinawa: 90.
  About contents of the address
  Because it will deliver to China, Japan by our Post Office returned address (Sender’s Name), sender information will be our Postage license Number and returned address.
Japanese clients could print their company name and URL on envelope, but it can not be same as China and Japan famous company’s name.
Please notice, your company telephone and address can not print on envelpe or big size DM.
Report
  About returned printing matters, if client provided incorrect address or addresses was changed, we will record all these returned printing matters in excel file and send it to client in China or Japan.
